What is elCAMPING

el CAMPING is an open plateform and community for camping-loving people. We share our passion around the world to encourage people to go meet nature and rediscover theirselves.
This plateform facilitates for groups and particulars, to meet-up and to get prepared for their next adventure.
We are envirement-friends and we do not discriminate.
